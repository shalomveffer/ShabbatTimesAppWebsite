Shabbat Times

Candle lighting and havdalah times

Shabbat Times gives you a clean, uncluttered view of candle lighting and Shabbat end times for your current location.

You can save additional locations to keep an eye on Shabbat times for friends, family, and places you care about.

What you can do

  • See upcoming Shabbat times at a glance
  • Get a reminder at candle lighting time
  • Toggle between candle lighting and sunset times

Upgrade to unlock the full experience

  • Holiday times: See times for holidays, not just the upcoming Shabbat.
  • Time travel: Browse future Shabbat and holiday times.
  • Advanced reminders: Set multiple reminders, customize offsets, and manage alerts for multiple locations.
  • Location labels: Save places with custom names (great for friends, family, and frequent travel).

I hope you enjoy!

Privacy

Your privacy is important. None of your personal information is collected or stored.

The app uses your location only to calculate accurate candle lighting and havdalah times.
